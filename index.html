Game Overview

Enter The Mountain Called Monkey, a brand-new VR game focused on atmospheric exploration, tense encounters, and progression. You begin inside a quiet mountain cabin, discover a hidden hole, and descend into a area elevators to travel with. Explore, earn XP, unlock new mountains, and uncover the dark secrets that lie beneath the mountain.
